id - unique id for the agreement.
product_code - the code of the product which agreement belongs to.
client_id - id of the client which agreement belongs to.
interest - yearly interest rate.
term - months to pay off a debt.
principal_amount - principal amount of the loan.
origination_amount - origination amount of the loan.
status (NEW, ACTIVE, CLOSED) - status of the loan.
disbursement_date - date of the disbursement of the loan.
next_payment_date - date when client should pay off the part of the loan next time.
